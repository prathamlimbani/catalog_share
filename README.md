# 📦 CatalogShare — Digitize Your Wholesale Catalog

**Live:** [catalogshare.online](https://catalogshare.online) [suchiketu.tech](https://sychisetu.tech)

CatalogShare is a web platform that helps **wholesalers and distributors** create an **online product catalog** and share it with their customers through a **single link**. Customers can browse products, add items to their cart, and place orders directly via **WhatsApp** — no app downloads, no sign-ups required for buyers.

---

## 🚀 What Problem Does It Solve?

Most small and medium wholesalers in India still rely on **physical catalogs, PDFs, or WhatsApp image dumps** to share their product listings. This leads to:

- ❌ **Disorganized product sharing** — images scattered across WhatsApp groups
- ❌ **No centralized catalog** — customers can't browse products easily
- ❌ **Manual order collection** — writing down orders from calls or texts
- ❌ **No online presence** — small wholesalers can't afford custom websites or apps
- ❌ **Stock confusion** — customers order items that are out of stock

### ✅ CatalogShare solves all of this by providing:

- **One-link catalog** — share a single URL with all your customers
- **WhatsApp-based ordering** — orders are sent directly to the wholesaler's WhatsApp
- **Real-time stock management** — mark products as in-stock or out-of-stock
- **Category organization** — products organized by categories for easy browsing
- **Custom branding** — company logo, colors, and contact info
- **Admin dashboard** — full control over products, categories, and analytics
- **Zero cost for buyers** — customers don't need to create accounts

---

## 🔧 How It Works

1. **Register** — Wholesaler creates a company profile with name, logo, WhatsApp number, and branding
2. **Add Products** — Upload products with images, prices, categories, and stock status
3. **Share Link** — Share the unique catalog link with customers (e.g., `catalogshare.online/store/your-company`)
4. **Receive Orders** — Customers browse, add to cart, and send the order directly to WhatsApp
